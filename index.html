Welcome to Oaks of Righteousness Orphanage

Oaks of Righteousness is a non-profit orphanage committed to nurturing and supporting orphaned children. Founded by a dedicated advocate for children's rights with extensive experience in counselling and community development, the orphanage strives to provide comprehensive care, education, and emotional support to children in need. With a dedicated board of directors led by an experienced leader in nonprofit management, Oaks of Righteousness aims to ensure a brighter future for the children it serves.
