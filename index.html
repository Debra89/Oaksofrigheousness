Welcome to Oaks of Righteousness Orphanage!

Discover a Place Where Growth and Grace Blossom

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Savannah City, just south of Johannesburg, Oaks of Righteousness Orphanage is more than just a sanctuary—it's a nurturing haven where young girls blossom into confident, capable women of faith. Our mission is deeply inspired by the timeless wisdom of Isaiah 61:3: “They will be called oaks of righteousness, a planting of the Lord for the display of His splendor.” Here, we take this scripture to heart, crafting an environment that embodies both strength and beauty.

Our Promise to Every Child

At Oaks of Righteousness, we believe in nurturing the whole child—mind, body, and spirit. From the tender age of 2 through to their university years and beyond, each girl is given the love, care, and resources needed to thrive.

Our Approach Includes:

  • Holistic Care: Comprehensive health, emotional, and educational support.
  • Academic Excellence: Tailored programs and resources to excel in their studies.
  • Faith-Focused Growth: Instilling biblical values and principles through daily activities.
  • Empowerment for the Future: Preparing girls for university and the workforce with life skills and confidence.

Get Involved and Make a Difference

Whether through donations, volunteering, or spreading the word, your support helps us continue to nurture, educate, and inspire.
