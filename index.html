Welcome to Oaks of Righteousness Orphanage!

Discover a Place Where Growth and Grace Blossom

Discover a Place Where Growth and Grace Blossom Nestled in the vibrant heart of Savannah City, just south of Johannesburg, Oaks of Righteousness Orphanage is more than just a sanctuary—it's a nurturing haven where young girls blossom into confident, capable women of faith. Our mission is deeply inspired by the timeless wisdom of Isaiah 61:3: “They will be called oaks of righteousness, a planting of the Lord for the display of His splendor.” Here, we take this scripture to heart, crafting an environment that embodies both strength and beauty. Our Promise to Every Child At Oaks of Righteousness, we believe in nurturing the whole child—mind, body, and spirit. From the tender age of 2 through to their university years and beyond, each girl is given the love, care, and resources needed to thrive. Our approach is a harmonious blend of exceptional care, enriching education, and spiritual development, ensuring that every child not only excels academically but also grows with a firm foundation in biblical values. Why Choose Oaks of Righteousness? Holistic Care: Our commitment goes beyond the basics. We provide comprehensive health care, emotional support, and educational enrichment tailored to each child's unique needs, ensuring a well-rounded and fulfilling experience. Academic Excellence: We believe that education is a gateway to a brighter future. Our tailored programs and cutting-edge resources are designed to help each girl excel academically and reach her highest potential. Faith-Focused Growth: We integrate biblical principles into daily life, fostering a deep spiritual connection. Our faith-based curriculum and activities are designed to cultivate a strong sense of identity and purpose rooted in Christian teachings. Empowerment for the Future: We equip our girls with the skills they need to thrive in university and beyond. From practical life skills to career readiness, we build confidence and prepare them for success in every facet of life. Get Involved and Make a Difference Be a part of something transformative. Explore our site to find out how you can contribute to our mission and make a lasting impact on the lives of these remarkable young girls. Whether through donations, volunteering, or spreading the word, your support helps us continue to nurture, educate, and inspire. Welcome to Oaks of Righteousness—where every girl is a cherished member of our family, growing strong in faith and flourishing with every opportunity.
